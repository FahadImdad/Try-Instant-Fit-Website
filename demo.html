Common Questions

How long is the demo call?

30 minutes. We keep it concise and focused on your specific store and goals.

Do I need to prepare anything?

Just have 2–3 product images ready to share. We'll upload them live during the call to show real results on your own catalogue.

Is the demo free?

Yes — no obligation, no credit card. Just a helpful conversation to see if we're a good fit.

What does the proposal include?

A pricing quote tailored to your catalogue size and traffic, an integration plan for your platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, custom), and an estimated go-live timeline.

What if I'm not ready to buy?

No problem — we're happy to answer questions and walk through options even if you're just exploring.
