FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How accurate is the virtual try-on?
+
Our AI handles fabric drape, wrinkles, body proportions, and lighting in just a few seconds. Customers consistently say it's the most accurate virtual try-on they've seen — and we tune it specifically for fashion garments rather than generic image generation.
How long does it take to launch Scan & Wear?
+
Same day. Register your brand, add your products in the dashboard, print the auto-generated QR codes, and stick them on garment tags. Customers can start scanning immediately.
Do customers need to download an app?
+
No app download required. Customers scan the QR with their phone camera and the try-on runs in the mobile browser — works on any iOS or Android phone.
How is pricing calculated?
+
Flat rate: $0.125 per try-on. No setup fee, no monthly subscription, no minimum commitment. You only pay when a customer actually scans a QR code and uploads a selfie. Volume discounts are available for high-traffic brands — contact us if you expect 10,000+ try-ons a month.
Is customer data kept private and secure?
+
Absolutely. Photos are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers unless the customer explicitly saves their look. We are GDPR-compliant and all data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
