FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How accurate is the virtual try-on?
+
Our AI handles fabric drape, wrinkles, body proportions, and lighting in just a few seconds. Customers consistently say it's the most accurate virtual try-on they've seen - and we tune it specifically for fashion garments rather than generic image generation.
How long does it take to launch Scan & Wear?
+
Same day. Register your brand, add your products in the dashboard, and you instantly get a try-on link and QR for each one. Share the links online or place the QRs in-store. Customers can start trying on immediately.
Do customers need to download an app?
+
No app download required. Customers scan the QR with their phone camera and the try-on runs in the mobile browser - works on any iOS or Android phone.
How is pricing calculated?
+
Flat rate: $0.125 per try-on. No setup fee, no monthly subscription, no minimum commitment. You only pay when a customer actually opens a product (by link or QR) and uploads a selfie. Volume discounts are available for high-traffic brands - contact us if you expect 10,000+ try-ons a month.
Is everyone's data kept private?
+
Yes. The customer's photo is only seen by the customer, never by the shop. The shop only sees numbers, like how many try-ons. And your product pictures stay only in your account. No other brand can see them.
