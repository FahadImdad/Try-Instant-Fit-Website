Pricing FAQ

Why no monthly subscription?

We believe you should only pay when your customers use the service. This aligns our success with yours. More try-ons = more sales for you = more revenue for us.

Are there volume discounts?

Yes! High-volume customers get discounted per-use pricing. Contact us for enterprise pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, wire transfers, and can invoice for setup fees. Usage costs are billed monthly.

Can I try before I buy?

Absolutely! Book a demo and we'll show you a live working version with your products.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts. You can pause or cancel anytime. We earn your business every month.

What about Google Cloud costs?

All infrastructure costs are included in the per-use pricing. You never pay Google directly.
